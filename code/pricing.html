Premium membership gives you unlimited access to all space missions (100+), all astronomical calculators, real-time weather and stargazing APIs, daily news updates, weekly curated newsletters delivered to your inbox, exclusive articles, ad-free experience, and priority customer support.
How does the weekly newsletter work? +
Every week, our team curates the most interesting space discoveries, upcoming astronomical events, and educational content tailored to your interests. You'll receive a beautifully designed email every Sunday with handpicked articles, images from space missions, and exclusive insights you won't find anywhere else.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes! You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time with no questions asked. Your Premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no hassle.
What's the difference between weekly and daily news? +
Free users receive a weekly summary of the most important space news. Premium members get daily updates featuring breaking news, mission updates, discoveries, and astronomical events as they happen. This ensures you're always up-to-date with the latest developments in space exploration.

Yes! We offer a 50% discount for students. Simply email us at support@astrophysicshub.com with your valid student ID to receive your discount code. We're passionate about making space education accessible to everyone.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and digital wallets including Apple Pay and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.
